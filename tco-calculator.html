TCO Calculator

Additional Information

The Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) calculation includes the following components:

  • Property Price: The listed price of the property.
  • Down Payment (20%): An upfront payment based on 20% of the property price.
  • DLD Fee (4%): A fee paid to the Dubai Land Department for property registration.
  • Other Fees: Miscellaneous costs like agency fees, NOC fees, and trustee fees (set at AED 5,000 in this calculator).
  • Loan Interest: The interest paid over the loan tenure based on a 4.99% annual rate.
  • Total Loan Repayment: The principal and interest combined, paid over the loan tenure.
  • Monthly EMI: The Equated Monthly Installment calculated for the loan tenure.
